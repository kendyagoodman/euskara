Kasu Laborategia

Basque cases, demystified. Every case is named after its question word — learn the question, and you've learned the case.

A case ending is just an English preposition glued onto the end of the word. Bilbo → Bilbora = "to Bilbao". The question word — Nora? "to where?" — is the memory hook.
